Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is the yurts?
The yurts are a round, tent structure where you can stay comfortably in nature.
Why choose the yurts?
Yurts offer the perfect blend of adventure and comfort. Whatever you want to do, listen to the sounds of nature, relax in a peaceful environment, our yurts make you comfortable.
What are the features of the yurts?
Our yurts have comfortable bed, electricity, Wi-Fi, private decks, and air conditioner.
