Our Story & Founder

Pacific Trails Resort was founded with a vision: to provide a serene, nature-immersed escape for adventurers and peace-seekers alike. Inspired by the beauty of the Pacific Northwest, our founder, Luangsiyotha Macham, built the resort to share his love of the outdoors with others.

  • Established in 2010 with a commitment to eco-tourism
  • Family-owned and community-centered
  • Located on miles of scenic coastal trails

Our Values

We believe in taking care of both people and the planet. At Pacific Trails, we promote wellness, nature conservation, and a sense of community. Everything we do is guided by these core values to ensure our resort is a place of balance and peace.
